Undergraduate Project Consultants    >     Laura Juliano

Laura Juliano is a second-year student in the College of Arts and Sciences. She is currently pursuing a major in mathematics but has strong interests in the fields of philosophy, chemistry, and drama, as well. Laura enjoys studying and performing the art of public speaking; she holds the honor of being the 2002 OHSSL State Champion in Impromptu Speaking. Here at UVA, Laura is a member of the University Guide Service, is a player and officer for the UVA Women's Club Softball Team, an Honor Educator, and volunteers through Madison House. She also holds the honors of being an Echols Scholar and a Jefferson Scholar.
