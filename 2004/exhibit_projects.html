Designing Matter Grant Competition 2004

Designing Matter participants devised independent projects on matter-related themes and learned how to communicate their research plans in grant proposals. Proposals submitted for course credit by individuals or collaborative teams were also considered for funding, made possible through a generous donation in support of undergraduate research.

Project summaries for award winning projects are exhibited here, along with those receiving honorable mention and other strong proposals still seeking funding.
