MATRIX REDUX: An Advanced Poetry and Printmaking Workshop, led by Lisa Russ Spaar and Dean Dass

59 K

Quotation

What is the matrix?  Derived from Latin mater (mother, the womb), it is origin and form-giver, intercellular substance, master, mold, subtext, plate, hungering page.  Making forays into the gap (abysmal, indirect) between matrix and print-poem, we discover the matter of this book.

 

(Lisa Russ Spaar, in “The Matrix”, book printed, hand bound, and published in an edition of twenty by the UVA Matrix Redux course, 2004)
